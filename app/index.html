Executive Summary

PrintIO is creating a suite of developer tools enabling cloud printing, fulfillment, and logistics; disrupting the antiquated process of printing and mailing.

We consider ourselves the AWS of Printing, a $72 billion market here in the US and over $500 billion globally.

Our platform aims to serve 100,000’s of potential companies as either a monetization tool or as a necessary piece of their business infrastructure.

By integrating with PrintIO, companies benefit from a single point of access to the largest international network of commercial printers.

Our International network automates the on-demand fulfillment any type of physical collateral. We fulfill over 100 product lines including Apparel, Business Documents, Wall Decor, Stationary, Photo Products, and Checks.

To move printing infrastructure to the cloud, join us at PrintIO.

PrintIO Moves Print and Commerce Infrastructure to the Cloud

The Need

International Monetization
Businesses need to monetize their brand’s assets, content & users.

Brand Marketing
Businesses want to market with branded products.

Printing and Mailing
Businesses need to send printed items as part of their core infrastructure.

The Problems

Manufacturer Fragmentation
On-demand manufacturing is highly fragmented geographically & by product offering.

Tech Complexity
Customization solutions for on-demand products are expensive & time consuming to develop.

Print Infrastructure Costs
In house infrastructure requires high capex and experiences periods of underutilization.

Lengthy Integration
Manufacturers lack time, efficiency & tech know-how to scale integrations. Resellers lack resources to source products & integrate with multiple manufacturers.

Difficult to Monetize
The world’s brands, mobile apps & websites have difficulty identifying and implementing new methods of monetizing their content & user base.

Access and Pricing
Shipping to international markets requires significant scale & logistical expertise.

Our Solutions

Powerful and Easy Developer Tools
Our partners want more than just a simple API to integrate with. We provide a customer facing web widget, iOS SDK & Android SDK to enable easy integration of a feature-rich front-end.

Unified Cloud Printing Network
We have assembled the largest international print & on-demand fulfillment network accessible from one access point.

Price Disruption
Through an exclusive relationship with our manufacturers we have built-in a price guarantee for our partners. Manufacturers value our ability to aggregate costly integrations and afford us this ability.

PrintIO saves businesses time and money by simplifying operations, minimizing tech expenditures and introducing new monetization channels.

The Scalable Model: Our Automated Workflow

100% Custom-built technology.

Order Submission Engine

The Order Submission Engine is the driving force behind capturing and validating an order. This engine is behind our Partner API, Web Widget, and Mobile SDKs.

Image Processing Engine

After order validation, the Image Processing Engine applies image transformations. It also tracks and reports image issues, allowing staff and partners to fix problems that would stall fulfillment.

Vendor Gateway

From this point, the order is queued for entrance into the Vendor Gateway-- a system that converts the order from our internal model to the model the fulfilling vendor desires.

Accounting Automation Engine

Our Accounting Automation Engine tracks transactions, invoices partners, and enables reporting. This seamless back office allows our team to stay lean and focus on building.

The Sales Process

Efficiently Managing a Large Lead Funnel.

Fill the Funnel

Leads are generated through Alexa rankings, Internet Retailer, App Stores and Web-Crawlers.

Oranizing Leads

We categorize leads into buckets with an outsourced team. Our buckets help determine their needs are and that messaging resonates with them.

Our outsource team then finds contact details, using best practices developed at growth equity firms and inputs them into our CRM.

Our internal sales team reaches out to pitch our services and a partnership.

Leads are then passed to our onboarding customer service team - who send product samples and complete onboarding.

Education & Speedy Integration

We offer easy to use video and written tutorials on our technology, printing methodologies, logistics and other related topics to smooth onboarding into a self service process.

Friends of Hellopics

Our marketing program that uses tech and incentives to drive virality

How It Works

1Friends Choose Payout or Charity

Friends are offered to aid their favorite charity or take-home a commission based on the sales their address book generates.

2Friends Auto Promote Hellopics

Using our custom-built & user-friendly admin panel, Friends automatically email their contacts, post to their social media accounts, and set up an automatic MLM routine.

3Information Capture

Hellopics captures email addresses from their address-book and captures their friend's birthdays and related info from Open Graph.

4PrintIO API Integration

When Hellopics receives an order, the Order Matching Engine finds all applicable sales and schedules the balanced payments.

5Friend of Friend Virality

Friends are incented to sign up new Friends, earning a percentage of all revenue generated by them.
